About Us

Colors United Painting is an MBE-certified, family-owned, and operated business serving multiple counties throughout northern New Jersey. Although established in 2022, we have several workers with over 40 years of experience. In a brief time, we have developed a reputation for excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Key Differentiators

  • Interior and exterior painting, wall or ceiling repair, floor services
  • Professionalism, timeliness, and seamless customer experience
